## General Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Rushed AI?">
    Rushed AI is an AI-powered development platform that helps you build applications faster using Claude Sonnet 4.5 and Haiku 4.5 models. It provides intelligent code generation, project management, and real-time AI assistance for your development workflow.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Which AI models does Rushed AI use?">
    Rushed AI uses Anthropic's Claude models:
    
    - **Claude Sonnet 4.5**: Advanced reasoning and code generation
    - **Claude Haiku 4.5**: Fast, efficient responses for simpler tasks
    
    The platform automatically selects the appropriate model based on your task complexity.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is Rushed AI open source?">
    Yes! Rushed AI is an open-source project. You can view the source code, contribute improvements, and report issues on GitHub.
    
    [View on GitHub](https://github.com/brayanj4y/rushed-ai)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What technologies power Rushed AI?">
    Rushed AI is built with modern web technologies:
    
    - **Frontend**: Next.js, React, TypeScript
    - **Backend**: Convex (serverless backend)
    - **AI**: Anthropic Claude API
    - **Auth**: Clerk
    - **Payments**: Dodo Payments
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Pricing & Billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How much does Rushed AI cost?">
    Rushed AI offers three subscription tiers:
    
    - **Starter**: $19/month (50 credits)
    - **Pro**: $49/month (150 credits)
    - **Scale**: $99/month (400 credits)
    
    [View detailed pricing](/resources/pricing)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What are credits and how do they work?">
    Credits are the unit of consumption in Rushed AI. Each credit is worth $0.30 USD and is deducted based on your AI token usage.
    
    - Credits are calculated from input/output tokens
    - Different models have different token costs
    - Credits include a 3x markup over base API costs
    
    [Learn more about credits](/resources/credits)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do unused credits roll over to the next month?">
    **Monthly credits**: No, monthly credits from your subscription do not roll over. They reset at the start of each billing period.
    
    **Credit pack credits**: Yes, credits purchased through credit packs persist across billing cycles and don't expire.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I purchase additional credits?">
    Yes! You can purchase credit packs at any time:
    
    - **Small Pack**: 50 credits for $10
    - **Medium Pack**: 150 credits for $25
    - **Large Pack**: 400 credits for $60
    
    Credit packs are added instantly to your balance.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I change my subscription plan?">
    Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    We accept all major payment methods through Dodo Payments:
    
    - Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
    - Debit cards
    - Digital wallets
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I cancel my subscription?">
    You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Credits & Usage

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are daily limits and why do they exist?">
    Daily limits prevent unexpected costs and overspending:
    
    - **Daily Credit Cap**: Maximum credits per day (3, 8, or 18 based on plan)
    - **Daily Token Limit**: Maximum tokens per day (40K, 120K, or 250K based on plan)
    
    Both limits reset at 00:00 UTC daily.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens when I hit my daily limit?">
    When you reach either your daily credit cap or token limit, you'll receive an error message. You'll need to wait until the next daily reset (00:00 UTC) to continue using the platform, or upgrade to a plan with higher limits.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How can I check my credit balance?">
    You can view your current credit balance, daily usage, and transaction history in your account dashboard.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How are credits deducted?">
    Credits are deducted in this order:
    
    1. Monthly credits from your subscription (consumed first)
    2. Credit pack credits (consumed after monthly credits depleted)
    
    This ensures your monthly allocation is used before dipping into purchased packs.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Why was I charged more/less credits than expected?">
    Credit consumption depends on:
    
    - Which model was used (Sonnet costs more than Haiku)
    - Number of input tokens (your prompts)
    - Number of output tokens (AI responses)
    
    Longer conversations and complex tasks consume more credits. Check your transaction history for detailed breakdowns.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Platform Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What can I build with Rushed AI?">
    Rushed AI supports building various types of applications:
    
    - Web applications
    - APIs and backend services
    - Full-stack projects
    - UI components
    - Scripts and automation tools
    
    The AI assists with code generation, debugging, refactoring, and implementation.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I import existing projects?">
    Yes! Rushed AI supports importing existing codebases. You can upload your project files and continue development with AI assistance.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I export my projects?">
    Yes, you can export your projects at any time. Your code belongs to you, and you have full control over your projects and files.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How does the priority queue work?">
    The Scale plan includes priority queue access, which means:
    
    - Your requests are processed faster during high-load periods
    - You get preferential access to compute resources
    - Lower latency for AI responses
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What file types are supported?">
    Rushed AI supports common development file types including:
    
    - Code files (`.js`, `.ts`, `.tsx`, `.jsx`, `.py`, etc.)
    - Config files (`.json`, `.yaml`, `.env`, etc.)
    - Documentation (`.md`, `.mdx`)
    - Styles (`.css`, `.scss`)
    
    Binary files can be stored but may not be editable through the AI interface.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account & Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How is my data secured?">
    Rushed AI takes security seriously:
    
    - Authentication via Clerk (industry-standard auth)
    - Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
    - Secure database storage with Convex
    - No sharing of your code with third parties
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Who has access to my code?">
    Your code is private and only accessible to:
    
    - You (the project owner)
    - The AI models (for generating responses, not for training)
    
    We do not use your code to train AI models or share it with other users.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I delete my account?">
    Yes, you can request account deletion by contacting support. This will permanently remove your account, projects, and associated data.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I reset my password?">
    Password management is handled by Clerk. Use the "Forgot Password" link on the sign-in page to reset your password.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are the system requirements?">
    Rushed AI is a web-based platform accessible from any modern browser:
    
    - Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions)
    - Stable internet connection
    - JavaScript enabled
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I use Rushed AI offline?">
    No, Rushed AI requires an internet connection as it relies on cloud-based AI models and backend services.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What APIs does Rushed AI use?">
    Rushed AI integrates with:
    
    - **Anthropic API**: Claude AI models
    - **Clerk**: Authentication
    - **Convex**: Serverless backend and database
    - **Dodo Payments**: Payment processing
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I self-host Rushed AI?">
    As an open-source project, you can fork and self-host Rushed AI. However, you'll need to:
    
    - Set up your own Anthropic API key
    - Configure Clerk authentication
    - Deploy Convex backend
    - Handle your own infrastructure costs
    
    [View setup instructions](https://github.com/brayanj4y/rushed-ai)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
