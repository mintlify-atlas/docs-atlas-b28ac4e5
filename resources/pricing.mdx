## How Credits Work

Credits are consumed based on your AI model usage:

- **Monthly Allocation**: Your plan determines how many credits you receive each month
- **Daily Caps**: Prevents accidental over-usage by limiting daily credit consumption
- **Token Limits**: Additional safety limit based on token usage
- **Credit Packs**: Supplement your monthly credits with one-time purchases

<Info>
  Unused monthly credits do not roll over to the next billing period. Credit pack purchases are added to your balance and persist across billing cycles.
</Info>

## Changing Plans

You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time:

1. Navigate to your account settings
2. Select a new plan
3. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle

<Warning>
  When downgrading, ensure your usage patterns align with the new plan's limits to avoid service interruptions.
</Warning>
