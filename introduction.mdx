## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe Your Idea">
    Start by describing what you want to build in natural language. Be as detailed or as high-level as you like.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="AI Generates Code">
    Our AI agent analyzes your prompt and generates the necessary code, files, and folder structure for your project.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Edit and Refine">
    Use the browser-based editor to make changes, or continue chatting with the AI to refine features and fix issues.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Preview and Test">
    See your application running live in the preview pane. Test functionality and iterate quickly.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Export and Deploy">
    When you're ready, export your project to GitHub and deploy it to your favorite hosting platform.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Technology Stack

Rushed AI is built with modern technologies:

- **AI Engine**: Claude Sonnet 4 (Anthropic)
- **Frontend**: Next.js 16, React 19, TypeScript
- **Backend**: Convex (real-time database)
- **Authentication**: Clerk
- **Editor**: CodeMirror 6
- **Preview**: WebContainer API
- **Payments**: Dodo Payments
- **Analytics**: PostHog, Sentry
